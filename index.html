故宫 - 紫禁城

Forbidden City - The Imperial Palace

世界上现存规模最大、保存最为完整的木质结构古建筑群,中国明清两代的皇家宫殿

The largest and best-preserved wooden ancient architectural complex in the world, the imperial palace of the Ming and Qing dynasties in China

长城 - 万里长城

Great Wall - The Long Wall

中国古代伟大的防御工程,中华民族的象征,世界文化遗产

Ancient China's great defensive project, symbol of the Chinese nation, world cultural heritage

天坛

Temple of Heaven

明清两代皇帝祭天、祈求五谷丰登的场所,中国古代天人合一哲学思想的体现

A place where emperors of the Ming and Qing dynasties worshiped heaven and prayed for good harvests, embodying the ancient Chinese philosophical thought of harmony between heaven and humanity

颐和园

Summer Palace

中国现存规模最大、保存最完整的皇家园林,被誉为皇家园林博物馆

The largest and best-preserved imperial garden in China, known as the imperial garden museum

布达拉宫

Potala Palace

世界上海拔最高的古代宫殿,藏传佛教的圣地,融合藏汉建筑风格

The highest ancient palace in the world, a sacred place of Tibetan Buddhism, integrating Tibetan and Han architectural styles
